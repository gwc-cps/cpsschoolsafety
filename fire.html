FAQ

What to do when you are stuck in a room and don't know if there is a fire?

1) Check to see if there is smoke coming from under the doors, if there is DON’T OPEN THE DOOR. If you don’t see smoke touch the door. If the door is not hot touch the doorknob. If the doorknob is hot don’t go outside or you will die!!

What should I do when a fire alarm sounds?

When the fire alarm sounds, direct students to the safest exit and go to the safe areas. Stray students find a room, students must stay in your designated class! Take emergency supplies and include student roster. Don’t lock the door Stop drop and roll

What do teachers have to do when there is a fire?

Take attendance and document missing students
